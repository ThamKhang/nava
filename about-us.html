About Us

Who We Are

Nava Media is a full-service media production company based in Ho Chi Minh City. We craft stories that connect people and brands through film, design, and digital content. Our team brings together creative directors, producers, and editors who share one goal: turning ideas into powerful visual experiences.

From brand films to social campaigns, we manage every step — concept, scripting, production, and post — to ensure clarity, quality, and impact. We combine strategic thinking with artistic execution, producing content that is both beautiful and effective.

Our production process is built around creativity and efficiency. Every project begins with a clear brief, a strong idea, and a detailed plan.

  • Creative concept and storytelling
  • On-set direction and cinematography
  • Editing, color grading, and sound design
  • Animation and motion graphics
  • Distribution and campaign support

We believe collaboration is key. Nava Media works closely with each client, adapting to timelines and goals while ensuring creative excellence.

  • Brand and corporate films
  • Social media campaigns
  • Commercial and digital video ads
  • Product explainers and event coverage
  • End-to-end creative production
  • Content optimization and delivery
  • Continuous post-launch support
